About me

I have been involved in writing and publishing since 2000, when I began working full-time as a writer and educational editor for R.I.C. Publications www.ricgroup.com.au, a publisher of classroom resources for teachers. In this position, I wrote in every curriculum subject imaginable— including Creative Arts, Society and Environment, Health, Mathematics, English, Music and Science. I was also the editor of the company's magazine for teachers, Class Ideas K–3.

I am the author of chapter books, literature units and history resources for school-aged children with renowned Australian and New Zealand educational publishers. In 2016 I became an accredited editor, having passed a written exam administered by the Australian Institute of Professional Editors. I also hold a BA (English), DipEd (Primary), Graduate Certificate of Editing and Publishing, Diploma of Freelance Journalism, Diploma of Professional Children's Writing and an Associate Diploma in Speech and Drama.

I have worked as a primary teacher, speech and drama examiner, professional actor and zoo and wildlife park guide, so I have a wide range of experience to draw from in my writing!
